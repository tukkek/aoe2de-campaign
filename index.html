Instructions
  1. Generate new campaigns as needed until you are ready to begin.
  2. Green hexes are allies that will reinforce your team when you invade their neighbors.
  3. Go from the bottom green hexes to the top, choosing your own path, one neighboring area at a time.
  4. To conquer a hex, play a match against the AI. Hover with the cursor to see match setup details.
  5. After winning a match, click the respective hex to conquer it as a new ally.
  6. Victories reward you with gold! When the option is available, you may pay gold to add allies for one match.
  7. To save your campaign progress, use the permalink function below (then bookmark the tab, for example).
  8. You win the campaign after conquering any Extreme difficulty hex.
